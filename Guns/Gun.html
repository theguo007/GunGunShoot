Reload to get one bullet. Shoot loses you a bullet. Block deflects bullet. Bullets deflect bullets too. To win, you must shoot while the other person is reloading, or you can get five bullets and shoot through their blocks. But be careful, once you get five bullets, you must use them all at once, and an opponent's bullet may cause you to lose all five of yours! Created by Kevin Guo!!!
